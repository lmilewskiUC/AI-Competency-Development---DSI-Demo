Beginner skills prompt instructors to start thinking about their strategy for AI use in the context of the broader teaching skill. Activities focus on exploration, reflection, and goal setting.

Intermediate skills develop instructor competence in one or two specific areas within the broader skill, using AI to complete a teaching task thoughtfully and with full awareness of its benefits and pitfalls.

Advanced skills mark the full integration of AI into the instructor's teaching repertoire, cultivating a practice of using AI systemically to improve teaching and learning.
